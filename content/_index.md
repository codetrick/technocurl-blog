### Who am I

I am a 4th year curler who curls out of [Nutmeg Curling
Club](https://nutmegcurling.com) in Bridgeport, CT, USA. I have a doctoral degree in applied physics, and have been writing software to support research and engineering for more than a decade.

### Why am I doing this

This is a hobby project of mine. I would like to leverage the massive progress
made in recent years in the field of computer vision and deep neural networks
to:
1. Advance our understanding of the physics of curling.
2. Use video analysis to make watching curling more enjoyable (stats for nerds?).
3. Make curling training more quantitative and science-based.
